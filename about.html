About PDF Baker

PDF Baker is a free, fast, and secure image-to-PDF converter that works entirely in your browser. No uploads, no registration, no limits - just simple, efficient PDF conversion.

Key Features

  • 100% client-side processing - your files never leave your device
  • Support for multiple image formats (JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP)
  • Batch conversion - process multiple images at once
  • Customizable page sizes and orientations
  • Mobile-friendly interface with drag & drop support
  • No file size limits or watermarks

Technology

Built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Uses the jsPDF library for reliable PDF generation. Designed with Pompidou Centre's high-tech architectural philosophy - where function determines form.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. All image processing happens locally in your browser. No data is transmitted to our servers. No tracking, no analytics, no data collection.
