Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

PDF Baker is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our approach to data handling and privacy protection.

YOUR FILES NEVER LEAVE YOUR DEVICE. ALL PROCESSING IS DONE LOCALLY IN YOUR BROWSER.

Data Collection

No Personal Data Collection

PDF Baker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require registration, email addresses, or any form of identification.

No File Storage

Your images and generated PDF files are processed entirely within your browser. No files are uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere outside your device.

No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any other monitoring technologies. Your usage patterns and behavior are completely private.

How It Works

PDF Baker uses client-side JavaScript and the jsPDF library to convert images to PDF format. All processing happens locally in your browser using your device's computing power.

Your browser's built-in security features ensure that PDF Baker cannot access files outside of those you explicitly select for conversion.

Third-Party Services

We use Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load the jsPDF library. These services may log basic connection information (IP address, user agent) but this data is not accessible to or controlled by PDF Baker.

Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, the security of your files depends on your device's security. We recommend keeping your browser updated and using devices with appropriate security measures.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page. Given our zero-data-collection approach, policy changes are unlikely but will be communicated if they occur.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can find our contact information on the About page.
