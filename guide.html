User Guide

Learn how to use PDF Baker to convert your images to PDF files quickly and easily.

How to Convert Images to PDF

  1. Select Images: Click the upload area or drag and drop your image files. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP.
  2. Review Selection: Preview your selected images. You can remove individual images by clicking the red remove button.
  3. Configure Settings: Choose your preferred page size (A4, A3, Letter) and orientation (Portrait or Landscape).
  4. Create PDF: Click the "Create PDF" button. The progress bar will show conversion status.
  5. Download: Your PDF will automatically download when ready. The file will be named with a timestamp for easy identification.

TIP: You can select multiple images at once for batch conversion. The order of images in the PDF will match the order they appear in the preview.

Troubleshooting

Large Files: Very large images may take longer to process. Consider resizing images before conversion for faster results.

Browser Issues: If conversion fails, try refreshing the page and ensure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

Mobile Usage: On mobile devices, you can access your camera directly from the file selector to convert photos instantly.

Supported Formats

Input: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
Output: PDF (Portable Document Format)
Page Sizes: A4, A3, Letter, Auto-fit
Orientations: Portrait, Landscape
